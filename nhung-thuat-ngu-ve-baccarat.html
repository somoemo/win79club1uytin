Baccarat duoc choi tren mot ban co kich thuoc tuong doi lon va duoc chia thanh nhieu khu vuc dat cuoc khac nhau. Ban co the dat cuoc vao bat ky mot trong ba khu vuc nay de du doan xem ai se co diem so cao hon trong van choi.

3. Cuoc Player va Banker

Khi choi Baccarat, ban co the dat cuoc vao khu vuc Player hoac Banker de du doan nguoi co diem so cao hon. Neu ban dat cuoc vao Player va nguoi choi thang, ban se nhan duoc tien thang gap doi so tien dat cuoc. Tuy nhien, neu ban dat cuoc vao Banker va nguoi dai dien cho nha cai thang, ban se duoc tra tien thang voi ty le 1:1 va se phai tra khoan phi 5% cho nha cai. Dieu nay la de bu dap cho viec Banker co ti le thang cao hon Player trong moi van choi.

4. Cuoc Tie

Cuoc Tie la khi ban du doan rang hai ben se co cung diem so va ket qua cua van choi la hoa. Tuy nhien, ty le thang cho cuoc nay rat thap va chi co 8:1. Do do, day la mot cuoc rat rui ro va khong nen duoc su dung nhieu trong chien thuat choi Baccarat.

5. Natural va Third Card Rule

Trong Baccarat, neu ban hoac nguoi dai dien cho nha cai co tong diem la 8 hoac 9, thi do duoc goi la Natural va van choi se ket thuc ngay lap tuc. Neu ca hai ben deu khong co tong diem la 8 hoac 9, thi co the se can phai rut them la bai thu ba theo Third Card Rule. Quy tac nay duoc ap dung de xac dinh xem lieu can rut them bai hay khong va neu can, thi ben nao duoc rut bai do.
