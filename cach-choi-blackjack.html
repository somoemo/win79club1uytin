Xem Chi Tiet Bai Viet Tai: Cach Choi Blackjack

1. Quy tac choi Blackjack

Truoc khi bat dau choi Blackjack, ban can phai hieu ro cac quy tac co ban cua tro choi nay. Blackjack duoc choi voi mot bo bai gom 52 la, muc dich cua nguoi choi la co duoc tong diem gan bang hoac bang 21 va cao hon diem cua nha cai. Neu nguoi choi co tong diem lon hon 21, se thua ngay lap tuc.

Trong tro choi nay, cac la bai tu 2 den 10 co gia tri tuong ung voi so diem tren do, cac la bai J, Q, K co gia tri la 10 diem va la bai Ach co the duoc tinh la 1 hoac 11 diem tuy vao tinh huong.



Sau khi nhan duoc hai la bai dau tien, nguoi choi co the chon them la bai (hit) hoac khong lay them (stand). Neu tong diem cua nguoi choi gan bang 21, nguoi choi co the cuoc them tien (double down) hoac chia doi (split) neu hai la bai ban dau co gia tri bang nhau. Sau do, nha cai se tiep tuc rut bai cho den khi dat tong diem 17 tro len.

2. Chien thuat choi Blackjack

De co co hoi thang lon trong Blackjack, nguoi choi can ap dung mot so chien thuat choi hieu qua. Duoi day la nhung chien thuat co ban ma ban co the ap dung khi choi Blackjack:

  • Diem toi da: Neu tong diem cua ban la 11 hoac thap hon, hay luon chon lay them la bai de tang tong diem len gan 21.
  • Diem trung binh: Neu tong diem cua ban la tu 12 den 16, hay xem xet tinh hinh tren ban va quyet dinh cach choi dua tren tong diem cua nha cai. Neu tong diem cua nha cai la 7 tro len, hay lay them la bai. Nguoc lai, neu tong diem cua nha cai la 4 den 6, hay stand. Neu tong diem cua nha cai la 2 hoac 3, hay hit.
  • Diem cao: Neu tong diem cua ban la 17 tro len, hay stand va cho xem ket qua.
  • Chia doi: Neu ban co hai la bai cung gia tri, hay chia doi va danh tiep voi hai tay khac nhau. Tuy nhien, khong nen chia doi cac la bai co gia tri 10 hoac cac la bai co gia tri khac nhau nhu 10 va 6.
  • Double down: Neu tong diem cua ban la 10 hoac 11, hay cuoc them tien va chi nhan duoc mot la bai duy nhat. Dieu nay tang co hoi thang lon cua ban neu nha cai rut bai khong tot.
